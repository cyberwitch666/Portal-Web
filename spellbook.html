About the Spellbook

Portal.Web will culminate in a digital/physical grimoire & spellbook that will be available in May/June 2022.

The workbook will include readings, spells, archival materials, artwork made and collected over this yearlong period of cyberwitch experimentation.

There will be a monthly reader released with excerpts from the workbook along with information on upcoming events.

Physical copies of the workbook will be sold (covering physical costs of the production). The full digital workbook will be released for free/donation and an audio version will be available for accessibility.
